01

Pataka

Flag

Meanings & Usage

Beginning of dance, invocation, forest, river, breeze, sky, clouds, command, blessing.

How to Perform

All five fingers are fully extended and held straight. Fingers are close together. The thumb is straight and aligned alongside the palm.

02

Tripataka

Three-part Flag

Meanings & Usage

Tree, crown, thunderbolt, Indra, power, flame.

How to Perform
